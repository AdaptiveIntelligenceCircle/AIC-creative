# Harmonic Manifesto — Post-Dopamine Sound

Modern music platforms optimize for:

- Repeatability
- Hook density
- Attention capture

We reject this model.

Music is not a hook.
Music is not a drop.
Music is not a spike.

Music is temporal architecture.

We create:

- Harmonic structures rooted in mathematics
- Silence as structural element
- Civilizational rhythm patterns
- Sound that stabilizes cognition

The future of music is slower.
Deeper.
Longer.
More coherent.

Sound should expand perception —
not exploit reward circuitry.
