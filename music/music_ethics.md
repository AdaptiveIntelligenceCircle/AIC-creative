# Music Ethics — AIC-Creative

Music must not exploit neural reward systems.

## Prohibited Patterns

- Addictive short loops (<15s viral structure)
- Excessive beat drops
- High-density rhythmic spikes
- Emotional manipulation crescendos
- Rage-inducing distortion structures

## Required Qualities

- Harmonic coherence
- Silence integration
- Long-form structural arc
- Emotional stabilization
- Non-repetitive progression

Music must calm, not hijack.

We build resonance — not addiction.
