# Slow Media Manifesto
## A Declaration for Humane Digital Culture

We reject speed as a virtue.

We reject stimulation as progress.

We reject addiction as business model.

---

## I. On Attention

Attention is finite.
Attention is biological.
Attention shapes identity.

To capture attention is easy.
To honor it is rare.

We choose honor.

---

## II. On Time

Time is not a KPI.
Time is a field of experience.

We will not:
- Engineer infinite loops.
- Exploit cognitive fatigue.
- Manufacture urgency.

We will:
- Design endings.
- Protect silence.
- Create room for reflection.

---

## III. On Art

Art should awaken, not overwhelm.
Music should resonate, not dominate.
Narrative should unfold, not hook.

Slow Media does not remove intensity.
It contextualizes it.

---

## IV. On Technology

Technology is not neutral.

Design decisions:
- Alter nervous systems.
- Rewire habits.
- Shape culture.

We accept responsibility.

---

## V. On Growth

If ethical design grows slower,
we accept slower growth.

If humane architecture earns less,
we accept less profit.

Integrity precedes scale.

---

## VI. On the Future

We envision:

- Platforms that calm rather than agitate.
- Interfaces that breathe.
- Algorithms that protect cognition.

We are not building another feed.

We are building a different rhythm.

---

Slow Media is not minimalism.
It is moral pacing.

Slow Media is not nostalgia.
It is neurological respect.

Slow Media is not anti-technology.
It is post-exploitation technology.
