# Mitigation Playbook

For each risk type:

---

## Infinite Continuation Risk

Mitigation:
- Hard content boundary.
- Reflective pause.
- Manual resume requirement.

---

## Variable Reward Risk

Mitigation:
- Fixed predictable outcomes.
- Remove randomness in reinforcement.

---

## Escalation Bias Risk

Mitigation:
- Intensity cap.
- Balanced recommendation weighting.

---

## Excessive Session Risk

Mitigation:
- Time reminder.
- Suggest break.
- Reduce stimulation amplitude.

Mitigation must be structural, not cosmetic.
