# Cognitive Harm Matrix

Maps design patterns to cognitive impact.

---

## Categories

Low – Mild fatigue  
Moderate – Reduced reflection  
High – Compulsion risk  
Severe – Addictive pattern formation  

---

## Harm Dimensions

- Overstimulation
- Sleep disruption
- Anxiety amplification
- Dependency formation
- Identity distortion

---

## Mitigation Requirement

Each High or Severe classification must include:

- Dampening mechanism
- Session limit
- Exit pathway
- Transparency notice

Unchecked harm is governance failure.
