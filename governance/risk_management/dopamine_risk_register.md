# Dopamine Risk Register
## Systematic Monitoring of Compulsion Risk

This register tracks all known mechanisms that increase
dopamine-driven compulsion behavior.

Each feature must be evaluated against this register.

---

## Risk Categories

### R1 – Variable Reward Loop
Unpredictable reinforcement schedule.

### R2 – Infinite Continuation
No defined stopping point.

### R3 – Streak Pressure
Loss aversion tied to daily use.

### R4 – Escalating Novelty Curve
Increasing intensity over time.

### R5 – Urgency Fabrication
Artificial scarcity or time pressure.

### R6 – Social Comparison Trigger
Leaderboard or performance ranking.

---

## Risk Scoring Model

Each feature receives:

- Severity (1–5)
- Exposure Frequency (1–5)
- Recovery Buffer Present (Yes/No)

Total Risk Index:
Severity × Exposure – RecoveryBufferFactor

If score > 12:
Feature must be blocked or redesigned.

---

## Mitigation Requirements

For each identified risk:

- Define dampening mechanism.
- Define user awareness prompt.
- Define exit pathway.

---

This register is living.
All contributors must update it when introducing new mechanics.
