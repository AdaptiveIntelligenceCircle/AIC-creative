# Attention Safety Standard v1.0

This defines measurable safety criteria.

---

## Core Indices

1. Dopamine Risk Index (DRI)
2. Temporal Integrity Score (TIS)
3. Stimulation Density Index (SDI)
4. Compulsion Loop Coefficient (CLC)

---

## Acceptable Thresholds

DRI < 12  
TIS > 0.75  
SDI < 0.65 sustained  
CLC < 0.4  

Exceeding thresholds triggers review.

---

## Certification Levels

Level I – No infinite continuation  
Level II – No variable reward systems  
Level III – Full constitutional enforcement  
Level IV – Transparent metrics dashboard  

---

- Internal monthly scan
- Quarterly governance review
- Annual independent audit
