# Aesthetic Constraints
## Preventing Overstimulation by Design

Aesthetics influence nervous system activation.

This document defines mandatory constraints.

---

## 1. Motion Regulation

- No continuous high-speed motion.
- No flashing or rapid contrast oscillation.
- Animation easing must favor calm curves.

---

## 2. Color Policy

- Avoid prolonged high-saturation red/orange dominance.
- Night mode mandatory after 22:00.
- Contrast must support readability without glare.

---

## 3. Sound Governance

- No sudden volume spikes.
- No autoplay with audio.
- Maximum BPM threshold for extended sessions.

---

## 4. Visual Density Control

- Minimum whitespace ratio enforced.
- No stacked notifications.
- No visual clutter escalation.

---

## 5. Silence Quota

Every experience must include intentional stillness.

Silence is a design element.
