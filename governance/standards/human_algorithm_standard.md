# Humane Algorithm Standard

Algorithms shape attention flow.

This standard governs all recommendation,
ranking, and generative selection systems.

---

## 1. Objective Function Restriction

Algorithms must NOT optimize solely for:

- Session length
- Click-through rate
- Return frequency

Primary objective must include:

- Depth of engagement
- Cognitive sustainability
- Emotional balance

---

## 2. Intensity Escalation Ban

Algorithms may not:

- Escalate arousal progressively.
- Bias toward extreme content.
- Amplify emotional volatility.

---

## 3. Transparency Requirement

Each recommendation must:

- Provide explainable reason.
- Avoid opaque hyper-personalization.
- Offer alternative slower path.

---

## 4. Entropy Balancing

Novelty injection must be:

- Gradual
- Reversible
- Measured

Sudden intensity spikes are prohibited.

---

## 5. Review Trigger

If average session duration increases >20% after update:
Mandatory ethical audit.

Growth must not signal hidden compulsion.
