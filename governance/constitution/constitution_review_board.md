# Constitutional Review Board (CRB)

The CRB ensures the platform does not drift toward
engagement-maximization culture.

---

## Composition

Minimum 4 members:

- 1 Senior Engineer
- 1 Experience Designer
- 1 Cognitive Science Advisor
- 1 Ethics Lead

Optional:
- External independent observer

---

## Responsibilities

- Review all major feature releases.
- Audit Dopamine Risk Register updates.
- Investigate escalation reports.
- Publish quarterly integrity summary.

---

## Authority

The CRB can:

- Block a release.
- Require redesign.
- Trigger emergency rollback.
- Initiate ethical audit.

Product velocity does not override CRB authority.

---

## Transparency

Quarterly reports must include:

- Attention Risk Trends
- Session Duration Metrics
- Stimulation Index Distribution
- Incident Count

Governance exists to protect long-term integrity over short-term growth.
