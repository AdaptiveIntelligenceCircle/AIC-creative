# Amendment Process
## Evolving Without Ethical Drift

The Attention Constitution is foundational.
It must evolve carefully and transparently.

---

## 1. Amendment Eligibility

An amendment may be proposed if:

- New research reveals cognitive harm.
- A technical loophole enables manipulation.
- Industry conditions significantly change.
- Governance board identifies structural weakness.

No amendment may weaken:
- Non-Extraction Principle
- Anti-Addiction Architecture
- Temporal Integrity

---

## 2. Proposal Requirements

Each proposal must include:

- Rationale
- Impact Assessment
- Dopamine Risk Recalculation
- Alternative solutions considered
- Public-facing explanation

---

## 3. Review Stages

Stage 1 – Internal Technical Review  
Stage 2 – Ethical Review  
Stage 3 – Independent Advisory Feedback  
Stage 4 – Public Transparency Notice  

Minimum reflection period: 30 days.

---

## 4. Voting Threshold

Amendment approval requires:

- 75% Governance Board approval
- No unresolved ethical objection

---

## 5. Non-Regression Clause

No amendment may introduce:

- Infinite continuation loops
- Variable reward reinforcement
- Hidden retention optimization

The Constitution may expand.
It may not dilute.
