# Narrative Principles — AIC-Creative

Narrative is not content.
Narrative is cognitive architecture.

We reject:

- Shock-based hooks
- Rage amplification arcs
- Cliffhanger addiction loops
- Trauma exploitation
- Viral micro-story formats

We require:

- Long-form structural coherence
- Archetypal depth
- Civilizational framing
- Transformational arc
- Reflective closure

No abrupt emotional spikes.
No outrage farming.
No psychological hijacking.

Stories must expand perception —
not compress it into reaction.
