# 📜 ATTENTION CONSTITUTION

### for AIC-Creative

*(Art. Music. Narrative. Slow Media.)*

---

## Preamble

We acknowledge that:

* Attention is a finite resource.

* Attention shapes perception.

* Perception shapes personality.

* Personality shapes the world.

Therefore:

AIC-Creative exists not to seize attention,

but to protect and purify it.

---

# ARTICLE I — The Nature of Attention

### 1.1 Attention is not engagement.

Time on the platform ≠ value.

### 1.2 Attention is biological.

All designs must respect:

* The nervous system
* circadian rhythms
* Finite cognitive abilities

### 1.3 Attention is sacred.

All hidden manipulation must be prohibited.

---

# ARTICLE II — Non-Extraction Principle

AIC-Creative shall not:

* Optimize usage time
* Use random reward variables
* Create infinite scrolls
* Activate artificial FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)
* Conceal the content's end point

We are not building a casino for consciousness.

---

# ARTICLE III — Temporal Integrity

### 3.1 Every experience must have closure.

No infinite content.

### 3.2 Mandatory pause zones.

After the climax → silence.

### 3.3 No autoplay by default.

Silence is the default.

---

# ARTICLE IV — Depth over Stimulation

The system must prioritize:

* Depth > Speed

* Meaningful connection > Sensory stimulation
* Reflection > Reflexivity

If faced with a choice between:

* Impressing
* Or nurturing awareness

→ Choose nurturing.

---

# ARTICLE V — Friction as Care

Friction is permissible when:

* It helps users pause
* It helps users become self-aware
* It prevents unconscious consumption

Friction should not be used to:

* Increase retention
* Force behavior

---
# ARTICLE VI — Transparency of Intention

Every content recommendation system must:

* Publicly disclose its principles
* Not hide optimization motives
* Not personalize for manipulation

No “dark patterns”.

---

# ARTICLE VII — Biological Alignment

### 7.1 Circadian Respect

Night ≠ Day.

### 7.2 Nervous System Safety

No flashing, no constant overstimulation.

### 7.3 Notification Minimalism

Notifications are only given when they are meaningful.

---

# ARTICLE VIII — Anti-Addiction Architecture

AIC-Creative commits to:

* No competitive gamification
* No streak pressure
* No leaderboard
* No becoming a system of coercive habits

If the user leaves — the system does not try to pull them back.

---

# ARTICLE IX — Creative Responsibility

Art, Music, and Narrative are permitted to:

* Stir emotions
* Challenge perceptions
* Create climaxes

But the Slow Media Layer must:

* Neutralize if overly stimulating
* Create a space for integration
* Ensure recovery

---

# ARTICLE X — Right to Leave

Users have the right to:

* Stop

* Delete account
* Not receive notifications
* Not be monitored for micro-behavior

And the system must not:

* Send “We miss you” manipulatives
* Create feelings of guilt

---

# Enforcement Clause

If a module:

* Intentionally increases dopamine spikes
* Optimizes retention instead of depth
* Creates behavioral loops

→ That module violates the Constitution.

---

# The Oath

Every contributor must accept:

> I will not design to capture minds.

> I will design to cultivate them. >
> I will not optimize for compulsion.

> I will optimize for clarity.

>
> I recognize attention as a living system.

---

# Meta-Impact

If implemented seriously, AIC-Creative will:

* Be smaller
* Be less addictive
* Grow slower

But:

* Be deeper
* Be more stable
* Be more ethical
