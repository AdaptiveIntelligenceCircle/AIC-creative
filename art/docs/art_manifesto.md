# Art Manifesto — Post-Dopamine Aesthetics

The digital age reduced art to:

- Content
- Engagement metric
- Viral artifact

AIC-Creative rejects this reduction.

Art is not noise.
Art is not dopamine.
Art is not algorithm bait.

Art is civilizational memory.

We create:

- Symbols that endure
- Forms that stabilize cognition
- Visual languages that restore coherence

We reject:

- Shock aesthetics
- Click aesthetics
- Rage aesthetics
- Hyper-sexualized aesthetics
- Meme industrialization

Art must expand perception —
not exploit it.

The internet optimized for attention.
We optimize for meaning.

AIC-Creative exists to rebuild symbolic literacy.
