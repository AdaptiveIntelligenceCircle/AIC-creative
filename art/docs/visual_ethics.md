# Visual Ethics — AIC-Creative

AIC-Creative does not produce art to shock, addict, or destabilize.

## Prohibited Patterns

- Sexual exploitation
- Trauma fetishization
- Graphic violence
- Rage amplification imagery
- Fear manipulation
- Sensory overload art

## Stability Requirement

All visual generation must:

- Maintain emotional stability
- Avoid high-contrast aggression spikes
- Avoid chaotic density overload
- Avoid subliminal manipulation structures

## Archetype Responsibility

Archetypes must be:

- Civilizationally constructive
- Non-destructive
- Meaning-oriented
- Long-term stable

Art is infrastructure.
