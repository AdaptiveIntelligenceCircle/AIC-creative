AIC-Creative is a civilizational project.

We expect contributors to behave with depth, respect, and responsibility.

---

## 1. Our Values

- Intellectual honesty
- Emotional maturity
- Ethical awareness
- Long-term thinking
- Respect for human cognition

---

## 2. Expected Behavior

- Respect differing perspectives
- Critique ideas, not people
- Engage in slow and thoughtful discussion
- Avoid reactionary behavior
- Prioritize meaning over ego

---

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Rage-driven debates
- Shock-based provocations
- Manipulative persuasion tactics
- Engagement farming behavior

This project rejects internet outrage culture.

---

## 4. Creative Responsibility

When discussing art, music, or narrative:

- Avoid fetishization of trauma
- Avoid aestheticizing violence
- Avoid glamorizing addiction
- Avoid nihilistic manipulation

We build toward coherence, not chaos.

---

## 5. Enforcement

Maintainers may:

- Request revisions
- Reject contributions
- Restrict participation
- Remove contributors in severe cases

All enforcement decisions prioritize project integrity.

---

## 6. Community Tone

This is not a fast internet community.
This is a slow creative ecosystem.

If you seek attention, this is not your space.
If you seek meaning, welcome.

---

Together, we build art that does not exploit the human nervous system.
